Trail Making Test Instructions

This test measures your processing speed, attention, and task switching abilities.

Part A - Numbers Only

  • Connect the numbers in ascending order: 1 → 2 → 3 → ... → 25
  • Click on each number in sequence
  • Work as quickly and accurately as possible

Part B - Numbers & Letters

  • Alternate between numbers and letters: 1 → A → 2 → B → 3 → C → ...
  • Continue until you reach 13 → M
  • This tests your ability to switch between different types of information

Note: Your time will be recorded, and errors will be counted. Try to be both fast and accurate!
